Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2026
Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Leads QualiFlow ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our lead generation, qualification, and delivery platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our chatbot services, or purchase our lead packages.

This policy applies to all visitors, users, and clients across all 50 United States, with particular attention to state-specific privacy laws including but not limited to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), Colorado Privacy Act (CPA), Connecticut Data Privacy Act (CTDPA), and the Pennsylvania Breach of Personal Information Notification Act.

By accessing our platform or using our services, you acknowledge you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing address, company name, and payment information when you register for an account or purchase our services.
  • Lead Qualification Information: Responses to chatbot qualification questions, including but not limited to budget ranges, purchasing timeline, decision-maker status, company size, industry, and specific service needs.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team, including correspondence records.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time and date of visits, and interaction patterns on our website.
  • Device Information: Device type, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Chatbot Interaction Data: Conversation logs, response times, and engagement metrics from our qualification chatbots.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: As detailed in our Cookie Policy.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • CRM Integration Data: Information from your CRM systems when you enable auto-delivery features.
  • Lead Enrichment Data: Publicly available business information, LinkedIn profile data, company size estimates, and intent signals when you purchase our Lead Enrichment add-on service.
  • Payment Processors: Billing verification data from our payment processing partners.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To generate, qualify, score, and deliver leads according to your package selections and custom qualification criteria.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process payments, and provide customer support.
  • Lead Matching: To match qualified leads with clients based on specified criteria including territory, industry, and lead scoring parameters.
  • Communication: To send service updates, lead delivery notifications, invoices, and respond to inquiries.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve chatbot qualification algorithms, and enhance platform performance.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect our rights and property.
  • Marketing: With your consent, to send promotional materials about new features, add-on services, or special offers. You may opt out at any time.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (Where Applicable)

For users in states with comprehensive privacy laws, we process personal data under the following bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our agreement to deliver qualified leads and related services.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security.
  • Consent: Where required by law, we obtain consent before collecting sensitive data or using data for marketing purposes.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

5. Disclosure of Your Information

5.1 We May Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics services, CRM platforms, and email delivery services that help us operate our business.
  • Lead Recipients: Qualified lead information is shared with the clients who purchase the leads. This is the core purpose of our service.
  • Professional Advisors: Lawyers, accountants, and consultants who provide professional services to us.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5.2 We Do NOT:

  • Sell personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes (as "sale" is defined under CCPA and similar laws).
  • Share sensitive personal data without explicit consent.
  • Use lead data for purposes beyond those disclosed in this policy.

6. State-Specific Privacy Rights

6.1 California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold.
  • Request deletion of personal information (subject to exceptions).
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@leadsqualiflow.com. We will verify your identity before processing your request.

6.2 Virginia (VCDPA)

Virginia residents have the right to confirm whether we process their personal data and access that data; request correction of inaccuracies; request deletion of personal data; obtain a copy of personal data in a portable format; and opt out of targeted advertising, the sale of personal data, or profiling.

6.5 Pennsylvania

While Pennsylvania does not have a comprehensive privacy law comparable to the CCPA, the following Pennsylvania-specific laws apply:

  • Breach of Personal Information Notification Act (73 P.S. §§ 2301-2329): In the event of a data breach involving Pennsylvania residents' personal information, we will notify affected individuals without unreasonable delay and notify the Pennsylvania Attorney General if more than 1,000 residents are affected.
  • Unsolicited Telecommunications Act: We comply with Pennsylvania's Do Not Call registry requirements for any telemarketing communications.
  • Pennsylvania E-SIGN and Electronic Transaction Act: Electronic signatures and records are legally enforceable in Pennsylvania.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures including encryption (SSL/TLS for transit, AES-256 for rest), role-based access controls, multi-factor authentication, 24/7 monitoring, and annual staff training.

We will notify you of any breach affecting your data within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

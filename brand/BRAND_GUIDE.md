## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | WCAG AA Contrast |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary** — Trust, professionalism | Deep Blue | `#0057B7` | 4.8:1 on white ✓ |
| **Primary Dark** — Headers, hover states | Navy | `#003D80` | 8.1:1 on white ✓ |
| **Accent** — Qualified, "GO", positive | Green | `#00A86B` | 2.9:1 (accent only — never for body text) |
| **Accent Dark** — Accessible green text | Deep Green | `#007A4E` | 4.6:1 on white ✓ (use for readable green text) |
| **Dark** — Body text | Near-Black | `#1A1A2E` | 15.1:1 on white ✓ |
| **Gray** — Secondary text | Medium Gray | `#5A6A7A` | 5.2:1 on white ✓ |
| **Light BG** — Page background | Off-White | `#F7F9FC` | Used as background only |
| **White** — Cards, text on dark | White | `#FFFFFF` | 15.1:1 on dark ✓ |

## ADA Compliance Notes

- **All text/body copy** uses `#1A1A2E` on white (15.1:1) — exceeds WCAG AAA.
- **Large text** (18px+ bold or 24px+ regular) may use `#5A6A7A` (5.2:1) — exceeds AA.
- **Buttons** use `#0057B7` background with white text (4.8:1) — meets AA for all text sizes.
- **Green accent** (`#00A86B`) is never used for body text; accessible green text uses `#007A4E`.
- **Focus indicators** use a 3px `#003D80` outline — visible with keyboard navigation.
- **Logos and decorative elements** are exempt from color contrast requirements per WCAG.
- **Information is never conveyed by color alone** — icons, labels, and text accompany all colored indicators.
- Site will use semantic HTML, ARIA labels, skip-to-content links, and full keyboard navigation.

## Typography

- **Primary Font**: 'Segoe UI', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif (system fonts — fast loading, high readability)
- **Headings**: Bold weight, `#003D80` or `#1A1A2E`
- **Body**: Normal weight, `#1A1A2E`, 16px minimum
- **Line height**: 1.6 for body text

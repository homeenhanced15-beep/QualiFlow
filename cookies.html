Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Leads QualiFlow ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies, pixels, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies on our website and chatbot platform. This Cookie Policy explains what these technologies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding them.

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, login status, and other information. Pixels/Web Beacons are tiny transparent images that help us understand how you interact with our website and emails.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential/Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for our website and platform to function properly. They enable authentication, session management, and security.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your session state Session
auth_token Remember your login status 24 hours
csrf_token Prevents cross-site request forgery Session
cookie_consent Remembers cookie preferences 12 months

Legal Basis: Legitimate interest / Contractual necessity

3.2 Performance/Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website through anonymized data.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga, _gid Google Analytics interaction tracking 2 years / 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics rate limiting 1 minute
amplitude_id Product analytics for chatbot performance 12 months

3.3 Functional/Preference Cookies

Remember choices you make such as language preference or saved lead criteria.

3.4 Advertising/Marketing Cookies

Track browsing activity across websites for relevant marketing content and retargeting.

4. Consent Management

4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept all, reject non-essential, or customize preferences. Your choices are stored for 12 months.

4.2 State-Specific Consent Requirements

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings or via opt-out platforms like the Network Advertising Initiative or Digital Advertising Alliance.

6. Chatbot Cookie Usage

Our chatbot uses cookies and local storage for conversation continuity, lead scoring context, language preference, and session tracking. Chatbot cookies are classified as essential for our service.

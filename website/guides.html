CRM Integration Guide

Connect your lead flow directly to your existing sales tools. We support real-time delivery to Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, and more.

  1. Navigate to Settings → Integrations.
  2. Select your CRM from the list of supported providers.
  3. Follow the OAuth authentication prompts to grant secure access.
  4. Choose your sync frequency (Real-time is recommended for "Hot" leads).

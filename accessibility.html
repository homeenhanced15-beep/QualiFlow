ADA Compliance & Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: June 14, 2026
Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Our Commitment

Leads QualiFlow ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We continuously work to improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards to ensure our website, chatbot platform, and all digital services are accessible to the widest possible audience.

This statement reflects our commitment to compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act, and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

2. Accessibility Standards We Follow

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA: The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines published by W3C serve as our primary accessibility benchmark.
  • ADA Title III: We meet the requirements of Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act.
  • Section 508: We implement standards equivalent to Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.

3. Accessibility Features

3.1 Screen Reader Compatibility

Proper semantic HTML hierarchy, ARIA labels, alt text, and descriptive links ensure smooth navigation for screen reader users.

3.2 Voice Navigation

Full voice navigation capability using Web Speech API, including voice-activated chatbot interactions and navigation commands.

3.3 Keyboard Navigation

Full keyboard navigability with logical tab order, visible focus indicators, and skip-to-content links.

3.4 Captioning and Transcripts

Real-time captioning for chatbot interactions and captions for all video content.

4. Chatbot-Specific Accessibility

  • Screen reader announcements for new messages and status changes.
  • Keyboard-only operation for all chatbot interactions.
  • Voice input and output for auditory interaction preference.
  • Option to transfer to a human representative at any point.

5. Ongoing Compliance Efforts

We conduct quarterly accessibility audits, engage in user testing with people with disabilities, and provide annual staff training.

7. Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback on accessibility. If you encounter barriers, contact us at:

Email: accessibility@leadsqualiflow.com

We respond to accessibility requests within 5 business days.

9. Compliance with State Laws

We comply with the Pennsylvania Human Relations Act, the California Unruh Civil Rights Act, and the New York State Human Rights Law regarding digital accessibility.

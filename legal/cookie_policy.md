## 1. Introduction

Leads QualiFlow ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies, pixels, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies on our website and chatbot platform. This Cookie Policy explains what these technologies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding them.

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, login status, and other information. Pixels/Web Beacons are tiny transparent images that help us understand how you interact with our website and emails.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential/Strictly Necessary Cookies
Required for our website and platform to function properly. They enable authentication, session management, and security.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| session_id | Maintains your session state | Session |
| auth_token | Remember your login status | 24 hours |
| csrf_token | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |
| cookie_consent | Remembers cookie preferences | 12 months |

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest / Contractual necessity

### 3.2 Performance/Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how visitors interact with our website through anonymized data.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| _ga, _gid | Google Analytics interaction tracking | 2 years / 24 hours |
| _gat | Google Analytics rate limiting | 1 minute |
| amplitude_id | Product analytics for chatbot performance | 12 months |

**Legal Basis:** Consent (required in California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, and other comprehensive privacy states)

### 3.3 Functional/Preference Cookies
Remember choices you make such as language preference or saved lead criteria.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| lang_pref | Language preference | 12 months |
| criteria_pref | Saved lead criteria preferences | 12 months |
| territory_pref | Preferred territory/zip codes | 12 months |

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest / Consent (varies by state)

### 3.4 Advertising/Marketing Cookies
Track browsing activity across websites for relevant marketing content and retargeting.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| _fbp | Facebook/Meta ad targeting | 3 months |
| li_src | LinkedIn ad targeting | 3 months |
| gcl_au | Google Ads conversion tracking | 3 months |

**Legal Basis:** Consent (required in all states with comprehensive privacy laws)

### 3.5 Third-Party Cookies
Some cookies are placed by third-party services including chatbot analytics and CRM integration tools.

## 4. Consent Management

### 4.1 Cookie Consent Banner
When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences by category

Your choices are stored for 12 months. You may change preferences at any time via the "Cookie Preferences" link in our website footer.

### 4.2 State-Specific Consent Requirements

| State | Requirement | Our Compliance |
|---|---|---|
| **California** | Opt-out for sale/sharing (CCPA) | "Your Privacy Choices" link provided |
| **Virginia** | Opt-out for targeted advertising (VCDPA) | Consent required for marketing cookies |
| **Colorado** | Opt-out for targeted advertising (CPA) | Consent required for marketing/analytics cookies |
| **Connecticut** | Opt-out for targeted advertising (CTDPA) | Consent required for marketing cookies |
| **Pennsylvania** | No comprehensive privacy law; full disclosure | Full cookie disclosures provided |

### 4.3 Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control
We honor Do Not Track (DNT) signals and Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals. If your browser sends either, we treat it as an opt-out of non-essential cookies.

## 5. How to Manage Cookies

### 5.1 Browser Settings
- Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies

### 5.2 Opt-Out Platforms
- Google Analytics Opt-Out: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- Network Advertising Initiative: optout.networkadvertising.org
- Digital Advertising Alliance: optout.aboutads.info

### 5.3 Consequences of Blocking
Disabling essential cookies may prevent chatbot functionality and account login from working properly.

## 6. Chatbot Cookie Usage

Our chatbot uses cookies and local storage for:
- **Conversation Continuity:** Remembering where a lead left off.
- **Lead Scoring Context:** Tracking engagement signals for quality scoring.
- **Language Preference:** Serving the chatbot in the user's preferred language.
- **Session Tracking:** Preventing duplicate lead entries.

Chatbot cookies are classified as **essential** for our lead qualification service.

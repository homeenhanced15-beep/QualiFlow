Intelligent Lead Qualification Features

Advanced B2B Qualification

Our chatbots use a sophisticated BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) framework to qualify every lead. We don't just capture emails; we start deep business conversations.

Authority Verification

Bots identify if the user is a primary decision-maker, influencer, or researcher, ensuring your sales team speaks to the right person.

Budget Discovery

Non-intrusive budget range questions help prioritize leads based on potential deal size and project scope.

Pain Point Mapping

Natural language processing identifies specific business challenges, providing your reps with instant "talking points" for the first call.

Global Multi-Language Support

Go beyond simple translation. Our multi-language chatbots understand cultural nuances and industry-specific terminology across 20+ languages.

  • Native Translation: Not just literal word-swaps, but idiomatic, natural dialogue in Spanish, French, Mandarin, and more.
  • Region-Specific Scoring: Lead scoring logic adjusts for regional business norms and time zones.
  • Auto-Language Detection: The chatbot automatically switches to the visitor's preferred browser language.
  • Multilingual Dashboard: View conversation transcripts in the lead's language or auto-translated to English.

Automated Email Follow-Up

Don't let hot leads grow cold. Our system triggers customized email sequences based on the lead's qualification score.

Lead Type Sequence Trigger
Cold (Score 0-25) Sequence 1: Educational Nurture 1 hour after chat
Warm (Score 26-59) Sequence 2: Solution-Oriented 5 minutes after chat
Hot (Score 60+) Sequence 3: Instant Booking Alert Immediate
